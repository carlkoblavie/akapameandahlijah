Reviews

"The book succeeds in bringing together many local cases, alongside foreign ones, to explain major areas of commercial law in Ghana. Relevant statutes and, where necessary, constitutional provisions as well are analysed. The book contains a lucid exposition of the relevant law. It fills a major gap in Ghanaian legal literature and is thus very welcome"
Professor Justice Samuel Kofi Date-Bah
(Retired Supreme Court Judge)
This book is a timely piece of academic work which deserves high commendations, for it fills the long standing gap for a Ghanaian legal materials in Commercial Law
Prof. Christine Dowuna Hammond
(Snr. Lecturer, School of Law, Univeristy of Ghana)
When I saw this book, I instantly felt it was a Sweet and Maxwells publication until I looked at the spine and realised it was published in Ghana. The content of the book is timely and I urge all Judges, legal practitioners and law students to get a copy.
Piesie Nana Kofi Asante
(Deputy Rector of GIMPA)
